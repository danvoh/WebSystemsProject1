What is Omoinotake?


Omoinotake is a band formed in 2012 that started in Shimane, Japan and gained attention in the Shibuya/Tokyo regions. They are now based in the Tokyo region as of present.

The members are:

Fujii Reo (藤井怜央/レオ or Leo) - Vocalist & Keyboardist, Fukushima Tomoaki (福島智朗/エモアキ or Emoaki) - Bassist, and Tomita Hironoshin (冨田洋之進/ドラゲ or Drage) - Drummer.


The Music

Of course, I may be biased, but I do believe Omoinotake's music is no like other. On one hand it can be a very beautiful ballad of tones and sounds put together to tell a story and on the other hand, it can be something really uplifting and invigorating with rock and funky beats put along. They have made music for various media such as drama films, TV anime, and TV shows. With this, they spread their style and can easily adjust their music to perfectly fit many mediums while also sounding amazing.

At first, their upbringing was brought upon by doing street lives and any way to gain attention and fans. These street lives brought so many to huddle around and just listen on the busy streets of various cities in Japan. It went from the street, to studio performances, touring the country at festivals, and even an RV trip and performing in random settings like the mountains, a bathhouse, on top of roofs, and so many other aesthetically pleasing areas to accomodate the music (aka feng shui).
